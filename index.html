Our Story

Founded in 2019 in HMT, Srinagar, Umarabad FC is a grassroots football club driven by discipline and ambition. What started as a group of friends has grown into a competitive unit that trains hard, plays with intent, and represents its neighborhood with pride. We compete across local tournaments with one objective — to improve, to challenge, and to build footballers who understand responsibility, teamwork, and grit. Umarabad FC stands for effort, identity, and earning respect on the pitch.
