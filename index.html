Our Story

Umarabad FC is a grassroots football club that has become an integral part of the HMT Srinagar community. Founded in 2019 by a group of friends, we provide a welcoming and supportive environment for young players to learn and grow.

We participate in various tournaments at different levels, fostering a sense of camaraderie and pride within our community. Join us in supporting the future of football in Srinagar!

We train hard, play with pride, and represent our neighborhood in local tournaments. Our goal is to nurture talent and bring people together through football.
